My Story

Cakey Bakery is a home based small business where I bake wide range of homely cakes.
There are various chocolate and fruit flavors you can explore.
Baking has been my hobby since I was nine years old but with time I stopped doing it.
That is when the world shut down in 2020 due to covid and pandemic was declared.
Lockdown rekinled my hobby and helped me improve and create my own small business which I am super proud of.
I pray Cakey Bakery's venture will permeate and touch peoples lives through that which I create with my hands.
The essence of the cakes and food I prepae is in the passion and love I put into the product I create to arouse the senses.
